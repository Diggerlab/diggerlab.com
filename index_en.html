Diggerlab is a technology oriented Web startup founded at Tokyo and then moved to Shanghai in Autunm 2011.

Our interests are centered around Social Media, Mobile Computing, and Crowdsourcing. We believe the future Internet services will be a combination of computer power and human intelligence.

Our team consists of experienced engineers from industry and tasteful researcher from academic. We apply solid research findings in innovation practice.

MoboQ (Moboq.com) is a location-based micro Q&A service based on social media. It aims to provide you the ability to know what's happening anywhere in the world.

The design of MoboQ is inspired from real problems in our everyday life: when I need to know what is happening right now at somewhere away from me, what shall I do? Moboq provides a novel solution that utilizes the power of social media. Our algorithm searches for “possible answers at the given place right now” on the existing social media platforms (e.g., Weibo) in real-time, and deliver your questions to them.
