Moboq is a location-based micro Q&A application based on social media. Ask people there, now.

So.. what is SuishenQ?

Moboq is probably the pioneer location-based micro Q&A application. The design is inspired from real problems in our everyday life: when I need to know what is happening right now at somewhere away from me, what shall I do?Moboq provides a novel solution that utilizes the power of social media. Our system searches for “possible answers at a given place right now” on the existing social media platforms (e.g., Weibo) in real-time, and offers a trustful communication channel for the two parties. Help, and get helped from strangers, that`s our vision of SuishenQ: “the Kindness of the Crowd”.
