Diggerlab is a technology oriented Web startup since 2011. We’re based at Shanghai, China and Tokyo, Japan.

Our interests are centered around Social, Mobile, and Crowdsourcing. We believe the future Internet services will be a combination of computer power and human intelligence.

Our team consists of experienced engineers from industry and tasteful researcher from academic. We apply solid research findings in innovation practice.

SuishenQ is a location-based micro Q&A application based on social media. Ask people there, now.

So.. what is SuishenQ?

Suishenq is probably the pioneer location-based micro Q&A application. The design is inspired from real problems in our everyday life: when I need to know what is happening right now at somewhere away from me, what shall I do?SuishenQ provides a novel solution that utilizes the power of social media. Our system searches for “possible answers at a given place right now” on the existing social media platforms (e.g., Weibo) in real-time, and offers a trustful communication channel for the two parties. Help, and get helped from strangers, that`s our vision of SuishenQ: “the Kindness of the Crowd”.
